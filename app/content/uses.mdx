- [Notion](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/notion-notes-docs-tasks/id1232780281) - My personal swiss army knife for note taking/task management/scheduling
- [Shazam](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/shazam-music-discovery/id284993459) - Makes it easy to find songs when I'm out and about
